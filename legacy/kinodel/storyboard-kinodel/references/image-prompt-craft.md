# Image Prompt Craft for Storyboard-Kinodel

Compact prompt scaffold for `kind=i2i` story frames:

`[shot type] storyboard frame: [same subject from reference] [new action] in [specific place]. Preserve [identity/costume/style/palette] from the main frame. [Composition/camera]. [Lighting source + quality + direction + color]. [Atmosphere/textures/props]. Style: [style]. Mood: [emotion].`

## Camera cheats
- Wide establishing: use for world, scale, loneliness, pursuit.
- Medium shot: use for action, interaction, body language.
- Close-up: use for emotion, reveal, punchline.
- Extreme detail: use for important object, hand, eye, glowing device.
- Low angle: power or menace. High angle: vulnerability or absurdity. Dutch angle: chaos.

## Lighting cheats
- Neon noir: practical signs, wet reflections, cyan/magenta split, haze.
- Horror/comedy: single hard key light, deep shadow pockets, absurd bright prop.
- VHS/retro: soft tube glow, chromatic bleed, scanlines, dust, overexposed practicals.
- Epic fantasy/sci-fi: rim light, volumetric beams, glowing atmospheric particles.

## Consistency sentence
Always include one consistency sentence:
`Preserve the subject identity, costume silhouette, proportions, color palette, and material texture from the main frame.`

## Avoid
- Plot summaries: “This shot shows the cat getting revenge.”
- Empty adjectives: “beautiful, cinematic, cool.”
- Duplicate prompts with only shot numbers changed.
