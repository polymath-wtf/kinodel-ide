# Brief Gate Rules: Mid-Session Revisions

Session note: the user may revise the story shape after an initial brief is already accepted.

Key points:
- Treat later user corrections to shot count, location, or character-emotion mapping as authoritative.
- Rewrite downstream artifacts to the new count; do not keep an earlier draft just because it was already written.
- `main_frame` is a planning/rendering anchor, not always a copy of shot 1.
- If the brief says shot 1 is a low point (apathy/sadness), the `main_frame` can still be the calmer, more usable hero expression needed for downstream main-frame planning.
- For Kinodel, a compact correction like “5 frames, lofi room, main_frame gentle calm smile” should be folded into the brief and propagated to `story.json` immediately.
