# Video Prompt Craft for Filmmaker-Kinodel

A good video prompt directs change over time. It should never be only a caption of the input image.

## i2v scaffold
`First: [small motion]. Middle: [main action + camera move]. Final: [ending hold/reveal]. Add [physics/texture/environment].`

## flf2v scaffold
`Begin exactly on shot A: [start state]. [0-2s] [motion/camera]. [2-5s] [main transformation/action]. [5-8s] [arrive at shot B]. Preserve [continuity locks], ending exactly on the final frame’s pose/composition.`

## Camera moves
- Slow push-in: tension, intimacy, reveal.
- Tracking sideways: chase, travel, kinetic comedy.
- Handheld drift: grit, documentary, panic.
- Orbit: transformation, power, magic/tech reveal.
- Locked-off static: absurd comedy, horror, deadpan.
- Whip pan: energetic transition, surprise.

## Motion details that help models
- Weight transfer: foot plants, shoulder rotates, body leans.
- Object physics: bounce, spin, splash, sparks, glass shards, smoke curl.
- Cloth/fur: robe trails, jacket snaps, whiskers twitch, fur catches rim light.
- Environment: rain ripples, neon flickers, crowd recoils, dust motes drift.

## Avoid
- “Make the image move.”
- “Cinematic motion, beautiful.”
- Pronouns with multiple subjects.
- New dialogue/audio when audio is disabled.
