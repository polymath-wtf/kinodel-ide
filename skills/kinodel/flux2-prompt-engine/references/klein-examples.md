## 4. Comic & Sequential Art (Character Consistency Across Panels) {#comic}

> These four prompts generate consistent panels of the same character "Diffusion Man."
> Key: repeat the exact character description in EVERY panel.

```
PANEL 1 — THE CRISIS:
Style: Classic superhero comic. Character: Worried scientist frantically typing on glowing 
holographic keyboard, face illuminated by blue light showing deep concern.
Setting: Massive computer server room with sparking circuits and red warning lights flashing 
on monitors. Text: "The AI models are corrupting! We need Diffusion Man!"
Mood: Tense, urgent, dramatic blue and red tones.
```

```
PANEL 2 — TRANSFORMATION:
Style: Classic superhero comic with dynamic action lines and electric energy effects.
Character: Athletic 30-year-old with brown skin tone, short natural fade haircut with black 
hair, black-framed glasses, light blue button-up shirt. Body begins to glow with swirling 
gradients of deep purple, electric blue, and hot pink energy. Mathematical equations and 
neural network patterns flowing around him in glowing lines.
Setting: Small office with computer monitors displaying code and error messages.
Text: "When noise becomes signal, I am... DIFFUSION MAN!"
Mood: Powerful, transformative, dramatic backlighting.
```

```
PANEL 3 — BATTLE:
Style: Classic superhero comic with explosive action.
Character: Diffusion Man — athletic 30-year-old, brown skin tone, short natural fade haircut 
with black hair, sleek bodysuit with gradient from deep purple to electric blue to hot pink, 
glowing neural network emblem on chest, short gradient cape, purple half-mask, strong jawline.
Action: Extends both hands forward, shooting beams of structured noise at corrupted digital 
monsters made of glitching pixels.
Setting: Digital cyberspace with floating data cubes and cascading binary code.
Text: "Time to DENOISE this chaos!"
Mood: Intense, action-packed, bright energy flashes.
```

```
PANEL 4 — VICTORY:
Style: Classic superhero comic with warm, triumphant colors.
Character: Diffusion Man — athletic 30-year-old, brown skin tone, short natural fade haircut 
with black hair, gradient bodysuit from deep purple to electric blue to hot pink, glowing 
neural network emblem on chest, short gradient cape, purple half-mask, strong jawline, 
confident heroic smile. Giving thumbs up to grateful scientist beside him.
Setting: Calm server room, soft blue ambient lighting, orderly data streams.
Text: "You saved us, Diffusion Man! The models are generating perfectly again!"
Mood: Victorious, hopeful, golden sunset-like tones.
```

## 9. Character Consistency — i2i Templates {#character}

```
# Scene change (use portrait as input)
[Character] is now standing in [new location description]. Keep the character's facial 
features, hairstyle, skin tone, and body proportions identical. The character is now 
[new pose/action]. [New lighting description]. [New season/weather if applicable].
```

```
# Season + outfit change (use previous output as input)
Keep the woman's pose unchanged. It is now heavily snowing, the background is white and 
the trees are bare. Snowflakes are visible falling in the frame. The woman is wearing 
a black coat, and the umbrella is a yellowish-green color.
```

```
# Place couple in new scene (multi-ref: image1=location, image2=people)
The couple from Image 2 is now standing in the middle of the street of Image 1, 
holding the same object. Apply the style of Image 1 to them as well, make them blend 
in a smooth way into the image, keep image 1 colors.
```

## 10. Lighting & Weather — i2i Templates {#weather}

```
# Night transformation
It is now nighttime. The sky is deep blue-black. Street lights cast warm amber pools 
of light on the wet pavement. Keep the scene composition and subject unchanged.

# Winter transformation  
It is now winter. Heavy snow covers all surfaces. Trees are bare with frost. 
Cold blue-grey light. Keep all architecture and subject positioning unchanged.

# Golden hour shift
Change the lighting to golden hour: warm amber and orange tones, long soft shadows,
sun low on the horizon casting horizontal light across the scene. Keep composition identical.

# Storm approaching
Add dramatic storm clouds to the sky, keep the foreground and subject unchanged.
The light is now pre-storm: flat, grey-green, ominous.
```
