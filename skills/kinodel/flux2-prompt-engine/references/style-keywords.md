## 1. Camera & Lens Cheat Sheet {#camera}

| Term | Effect |
|---|---|
| **f/1.4 – f/2.8** | Blurry background (shallow depth of field) |
| **f/8 – f/16** | Everything sharp (deep depth of field) |
| **24mm** | Wide angle — shows more of the scene |
| **35mm** | Natural, documentary-style perspective |
| **50mm** | Eye-level, neutral perspective |
| **85mm** | Portrait-ideal, slight background compression |
| **135mm+** | Telephoto — strong background compression |
| **ISO 100** | Clean image, low noise |
| **ISO 1600–3200** | Brighter but grainy — useful for film-style looks |
| **Macro lens** | Extreme close-up detail |
| **Anamorphic lens** | Widescreen cinematic look with oval bokeh |

## 2. Lighting Keywords {#lighting}

| Term | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Golden hour** | Warm, soft, flattering — just after sunrise or before sunset |
| **Blue hour** | Cool, moody — just before sunrise or after sunset |
| **Overcast** | Flat, even, shadow-free — great for product shots |
| **Rembrandt lighting** | Dramatic triangle of light on the face (key at 45°) |
| **Split lighting** | High contrast, half-face illuminated (key at 90°) |
| **Chiaroscuro** | Strong light/shadow drama, deep blacks |
| **Backlit / rim light** | Subject glowing at edges, halo effect |
| **Soft box / key light** | Studio, controlled, even, commercial |
| **Practical lighting** | Light sources visible in scene (lamps, neon, fire) |
| **Diffused light** | Soft, wrap-around, minimal shadows |
| **Harsh direct light** | Strong shadows, high contrast |
| **Window light** | Soft, even, interior natural |
| **Neon backlighting** | Colored atmospheric glow, cyberpunk/urban |
| **Underwater caustics** | Dancing light patterns through water |

### Example lighting phrases (copy-paste ready)
```
soft, diffused natural light filtering through sheer curtains
dramatic side lighting creating deep shadows and highlights
golden hour backlighting with lens flare and warm amber tones
overcast light creating even, shadow-free illumination
single practical desk lamp, strong chiaroscuro, film noir aesthetic
neon signs providing atmospheric blue-pink lighting, wet pavement reflections
three-point softbox setup, clean commercial product lighting
Rembrandt lighting, key light at 45 degrees, triangle of light on the cheek
```

## 5. Composition Techniques {#composition}

| Technique | When to Use | Example Phrase |
|---|---|---|
| **Rule of thirds** | Natural, balanced | "composed using rule of thirds" |
| **Leading lines** | Guide eye through scene | "diagonal lines leading to main entrance" |
| **Foreground/background layers** | Add depth | "strong foreground element, background vista" |
| **Low angle (worm's eye)** | Power, dominance | "low angle worm's eye view, dramatic diagonal" |
| **High angle (bird's eye)** | Patterns, relationships | "bird's eye view, geometric overhead composition" |
| **Dutch angle** | Tension, unease | "dutch angle, psychological tension" |
| **Symmetrical** | Formal, architectural | "perfectly symmetrical composition" |
| **Negative space** | Minimal, product | "generous negative space, minimal composition" |
| **Frame within frame** | Depth, intimacy | "framed through doorway / window arch" |
